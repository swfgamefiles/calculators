Effects:
Efffects of ketamine come in varying levels. In low doses, the effects are comparable to alcohol intoxication. Users feel more energetic, slightly euphoric, and hava a sense that they are operating on 'autopilot'. Users may also feel more social.
In higher doses the effects are very different, effects include making you feel withdrawn and introverted. Users often report feeling very light and floaty and often experience intense hallucinations, blurred vision, and out-of-body experiences.
At very high levels, users experience what's called a k-hole. Effects of a k-hole include intense out of body experiences, a feeling of time being slowed, and a strong dissasociative feeling. Users compare this to 'peering through the keyhole of existence'. While this experience is exciting, the majority of users say the trip is more negative than positive.
